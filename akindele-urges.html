Hon. Akindele Adeniyi Urges Iju Residents to Stay Steadfast in Support of Ayedatiwa for Ondo Governorship Election
  • Mobarok Hossain
  • May 15, 2025
  • 4 Comments

Hon. Akindele Adeniyi met with the people of Iju in Akure North Local Government Area today, where he delivered a powerful message of encouragement and unity. He urged the community to remain resilient and unwavering in their commitment to Ayedatiwa in the governorship election set for Saturday, November 16th. Adeniyi emphasized the importance of this election and the opportunity it presents for the people to shape the future of Ondo State.

A Pillar of Service and Political Impact in Akure North

During his address, Adeniyi acknowledged the challenges and struggles faced by the people of Iju, recognizing the hard work and dedication that had gone into supporting Ayedatiwa's campaign. He expressed his admiration for their commitment, even in the face of adversity, and urged them to stay strong and steadfast. "Your loyalty and resilience are what will make a difference on election day," he said, encouraging them to see the bigger picture.

Famous News was present at the event, capturing the passionate speeches and enthusiasm of the crowd. Adeniyi's message of hope and determination resonated with many attendees, who were seen nodding in agreement and cheering in support. He reminded the people that, through their continued support, they could bring about positive change for the entire community. "Together, we can ensure a brighter future for our children and generations to come," he declared, leaving the crowd inspired and ready to make their voices heard on November 16th.
